makan

Makan is a website that allows users to advertise their apartments or houses, whether for sale or rent, as it specializes in real estate trading (apartments, buildings, shops, houses...etc.) The site offers two services:

• Advertising service: You can advertise your apartment or shop for sale or rent to thousands of users

• Buying or renting service: any user can find suitable apartments or shops for sale or rent without the need for a broker or paying any additional costs

Site features

• Easy access to houses whether for rent or for sale

• Equal chances for everybody to find a a proper place

• Easy search and customization to find what suits you in moments

• The users can use the site to rent their shops , Furnished Apartments , motels hotels ect..

People can find places to sleep in emergency cases without paying a lot of money or being exploited by brokers
